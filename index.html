About Us

Welcome to Boykin’s Finest Kennels,a family-owned haven in South Carolina where we pour our hearts into raising exceptional Boykin Spaniels.With a deep love for this remarkable breed,we’re committed to bringing you healthy,happy,and well-socialized puppies that capture the spirited charm and boundless versatility of the Boykin Spaniel.

Breeding Program

At Boykin’s Finest Kennels,our breeding program is a labor of love,centered on nurturing the finest Boykin Spaniels with exceptional genetics,robust health,and delightful temperaments.We meticulously select each breeding pair,ensuring they undergo rigorous health testing to meet the highest standards of care and quality.Our commitment to ethical practices shines through in every step—our dogs are cherished family members,raised in a nurturing environment where their well-being is our top priority.This dedication results in puppies that not only embody the breed’s classic traits but also bring joy and companionship to their forever homes.
